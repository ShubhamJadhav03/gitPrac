Prawns Masala Recipe

Ingredients:
- 500g prawns, cleaned and deveined
- 2 onions, finely chopped
- 2 tomatoes, chopped
- 2 green chilies, slit
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1 tsp coriander powder
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- Salt to taste
- 2 tbsp oil
- Fresh coriander leaves, chopped (for garnish)
- Lemon wedges (optional)

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a pan. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for 1 minute.
3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
4. Add turmeric, red chili, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
5. Add prawns and cook for 5-7 minutes until they turn pink and are cooked through.
6. Sprinkle garam masala and mix.
7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti. Optionally, serve with lemon wedges.

Enjoy your homemade Prawns Masala!
