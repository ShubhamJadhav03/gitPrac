Fish Tikka Recipe

Ingredients:
- 500g boneless fish fillets (e.g., salmon, cod, or tilapia)
- 1/2 cup thick yogurt
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp cumin powder
- 1 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste
- Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
- Lemon wedges (for serving)

Instructions:
1. Cut fish fillets into bite-sized pieces.
2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, cumin powder, oil, and salt.
3. Add fish pieces to the marinade and coat well. Cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes to 1 hour.
4. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F) or heat a grill/pan.
5. Arrange marinated fish pieces on skewers or directly on a baking tray.
6. Bake or grill for 15-20 minutes, turning halfway, until fish is cooked and slightly charred.
7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve with lemon wedges.

Enjoy your homemade Fish Tikka!
