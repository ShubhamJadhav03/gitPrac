Veg Lollipop Recipe

Ingredients:
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato), boiled & mashed
- 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
- 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 2 green chilies, finely chopped
- 1/2 cup bread crumbs
- 2 tbsp cornflour
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- 1/2 tsp red chili powder
- Salt to taste
- Oil for deep frying
- Ice cream sticks (for lollipop handles)

For Batter:
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour (maida)
- 2 tbsp cornflour
- 1/2 tsp red chili powder
- Salt to taste
- Water as needed

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, mix mashed vegetables, onion, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, bread crumbs, cornflour, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt.
2. Shape the mixture into small balls and insert ice cream sticks into each ball.
3. In another bowl, prepare the batter by mixing flour, cornflour, chili powder, salt, and water to make a thick paste.
4. Dip each veg ball into the batter, ensuring it is coated well.
5. Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry the lollipops on medium heat until golden and crisp.
6. Drain on paper towels.
7. Serve hot with ketchup or chutney.

Enjoy your Veg Lollipops!
