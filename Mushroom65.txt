Mushroom 65 Recipe

Ingredients:
- 200g mushrooms, cleaned and cut into halves
- 2 tbsp corn flour
- 2 tbsp rice flour
- 1 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
- 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1/2 tsp red chili powder
- 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
- 1/2 tsp garam masala
- Salt to taste
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- Few curry leaves
- Oil for deep frying

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, mix corn flour, rice flour, all-purpose flour, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice. Add a little water to make a thick batter.
2. Add mushrooms to the batter and coat well.
3. Heat oil in a pan for deep frying.
4. Fry the mushrooms in batches until golden and crisp.
5. Drain on paper towels.
6. In a separate pan, heat a little oil and fry curry leaves until crisp. Add fried mushrooms and toss well.
7. Serve hot as a snack or starter.

Enjoy your Mushroom 65!
