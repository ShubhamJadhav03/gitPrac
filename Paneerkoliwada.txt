Paneer Koliwada Recipe

Ingredients:
- 250g paneer (cubed)
- 1 cup gram flour (besan)
- 2 tbsp cornflour
- 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
- 1 tsp red chili powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
- 1 tsp garam masala
- 1 tsp chaat masala
- 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
- Salt to taste
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tbsp yogurt
- Oil for deep frying

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, mix gram flour, cornflour, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, chaat masala, ajwain, salt, lemon juice, and yogurt. Add a little water to make a thick batter.
2. Add paneer cubes to the batter and coat well. Marinate for 20-30 minutes.
3. Heat oil in a deep pan on medium flame.
4. Drop the marinated paneer cubes into hot oil and fry until golden and crisp.
5. Remove and drain on paper towels.
6. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.

Enjoy your Paneer Koliwada!
